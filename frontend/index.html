WELCOME TO 7R'S DECISION TREE SOLUTION

This dynamic guide is designed to simplify the complex task of choosing a cloud migration strategy for your legacy systems. Through a series of carefully crafted technical and strategic questions, the tool guides you through a personalized decision flow. Each answer leads to an optimized recommendation, based on the seven AWS application migration strategies (the "7 Rs"): Rehost, Replatform, Refactor, Rearchitect, Repurchase, Retire, and Retain.

At the end of the process, you'll receive a clear, well-founded suggestion for the most suitable strategy, maximizing the benefits of the cloud and minimizing risks. This ensures your digital transformation is successful and aligned with your business objectives.
